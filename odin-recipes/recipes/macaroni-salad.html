Classic Macaroni Salad

Description

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of uncooked elbow macaroni
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 1/2 tbsp yellow mustard
  • 2/3 cup white sugar
  • 1/4 cup distilled white vinegar
  • 1 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 stalks celery, chopped
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper, seeded and chopped
  • 1/4 cup grated carrot (optional)
  • 2 tbsp chopped pimento peppers (optional)

Steps

  1. Step 1:

    Gather all ingredients

  2. Step 2:

    Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook elbow macaroni in the boiling water, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 8 minutes. Rinse under cold water and drain.

  3. Step 3:

    Mix mayonnaise, sugar, vinger, mustard, salt, pepper, and macaroni together in a large bowl.

  4. Step 4:

    Stir in celery, onions, green pepper, carrots, and pimentos.

  5. Step 5:

    Refigerate salad for at least 4 hours before serving, but preferably overnight.
